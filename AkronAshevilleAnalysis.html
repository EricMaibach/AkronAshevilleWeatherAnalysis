Perform test¶

Calculate the test statistic

$z = \frac{\bar{x} - \bar{y} - \Delta_5} {\sqrt{\frac{\sigma_1^2} {m} + \frac{\sigma_2^2} {n}}}$

In [14]:
(akron["TAVG"].mean() - ash["TAVG"].mean() + 5)/math.sqrt(
    (akron["TAVG"].var()/akron["TAVG"].count()) +
